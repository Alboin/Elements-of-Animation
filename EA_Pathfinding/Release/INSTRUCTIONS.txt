The scene can be rotated by dragging with the cursor.
Zooming out/in can be done through scrolling.

Key W: Enable/disable wireframe.
Key P: Scroll through the free scenes listed below:
	1. Physical simulation
	2. Procedural
	3. Procedural + physical
Key F: Lock/unlock the fps (the fps can be seen in the window title).
Key left/right: increase/decrease the length of the procedural waves.
Key up/down: increase/decrease the height of the procedural waves.
Key C: Scroll through the different colors.
Key M: enable/disable fog.
Key R: enable/disable rain.
